Loan Features

I hand pick out the features that exists in both my historical data and new listing data retrived from Lending company api. The most important features are: Term, FICO Score, Loan Amount, Home Ownership, Dept to Income Ratio, Total Number of Accounts, Credit History, Employment Status, etc. Some of the feature engineering was done here, for example, I take the log of the annual income and reduce the number of categories of the home ownership, The credit history length is computed by the loan application date minus earliest credit line. For lending club, the joint feature is integrated into the primary application (basically boost his or hers features).
