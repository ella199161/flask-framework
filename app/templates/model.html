Loan Features

I hand pick out the features that exists in both my historical data and new listing data retrived from Lending company api. The most important features are: Term, FICO Score, Loan Amount, Home Ownership, Dept to Income Ratio, Total Number of Accounts, Credit History, Employment Status, etc.

Logistic Regression Classifer


In order to predict the loan risk, I used a Balanced Logistic regression model.

Most investors are aware, lending out money to other people come with a risk. The loan will start when the investor decides to take the risk. How about after the loan start? Should the investor expect the money coming back? Here, I used the Kaplan–Meier estimator and developed a real time loan survival model, basically computes the probability of an ongoing loan being paid off on time given its current activity. Therefore, investors have a batter idea of there money, and lead to smarter investment.

Model Performance:

This model out performs the auto invest option in the leading company’s default option, and each category, the charge-off risk is reduced by at least 10% comparing to the auto investing. Note that this is a high precision model. The cost for an individual investor to miss a good loan is low but accidentally funding a bad loan is high.
