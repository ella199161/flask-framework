Welcome

Many people have money sitting in a bank account seek ways to invest. Comparing to the rather unstable stock market, Peer to Peer(P2P) would be a great choice. P2P lending is the method of lending money to individuals or businesses through online services. On the investor's side, the investment return is much higher than bank CD account, and much safer than stock market.

I used data sets from the two industry lead companies: Prosper and Lending Club. I analyzed the P2P lending data for the past 10 years and run feature engineering and feature selection to construct a regression model to predict the interest rate of a given loan. On the investors’ side, I also developed a model to classifier whether a loan will be paid off on time or not as well as the risk associated to the loan. With my product, investors can make safer and more predicted investment.

About Peer to Peer Lending

When borrowers apply for loan, lending club and prosper assigns a grade, and each grade has its corresponding interest rate. Prosper offer grade: AA, A, B, C, D, E. and lending club offer grade: A, B, C, D, E, F, G. Note that the letter grade across the two lending company might not be directly comparable.
